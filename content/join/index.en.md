+++

Any member of TTY's member organizations can apply to become **an individual member** of TTY. Any person or legal community who supports the organization's activities can be accepted as **a support member**. Member applications are handled and accepted by the organization board. The membership duration is from the beginning of the calendar year to its end.

The organization can also have **honorary members** who have been commended on their work for the organization or otherwise have helped advance its goals in meaningful ways. Honorary members are selected by the organization's general assembly on the motion of the board and one cannot apply to become an honorary member.

## Joining as an individual or support member

- Send a freeform application email to hallitus@tietoteekkariyhdistys.fi containing the following:
  - your whole name (legally required)
  - official place of residence (legally required)
  - whether you want to become an individual member or a support member
  - which TTY member organization are you a member of (only if applying to become an individual member)
- Wait to receive the confirmation and acceptance of your membership by email
- Pay the membership fee according to the instructions given in the acceptance email

## Membership fees 2026

- **Individual Members**: 5 €
- **Support Members**: 15 €

No additional joining fee is required. Honorary members do not need to pay joining or membership fees.
