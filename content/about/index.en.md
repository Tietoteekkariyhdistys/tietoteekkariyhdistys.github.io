Tietoteekkariyhdistys (TTY) is an umbrella organization for all of Finland's IT technology student guilds, founded in 2023 during the Titeenien Taistot event in Turku to help the guilds communicate with each other, promote the interests of IT technology students, and organize events and fun for IT technology students all over the country.

The organization bylaws can be found [here](/bylaws) but are only available in Finnish (since they are an official document).

## Contact

You can reach the organization board by emailing hallitus@tietoteekkariyhdistys.fi.
