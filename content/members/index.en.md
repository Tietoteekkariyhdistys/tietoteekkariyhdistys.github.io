The actual members of TTY are all of Finland's IT technology student guilds which are student associations for university students studying to become Masters of Science in Technology with a major in Information Technology (or a related field).

- [Algo ry](https://www.algojkl.com/), University of Jyväskylä
- [Cluster ry](https://cluster.fi/), LUT University
- [Datateknologerna vid Åbo Akademi rf](https://datateknologerna.org/), Åbo Akademi University
- [Digit ry](https://digit.fi/), University of Turku
- [Oulun Tietoteekkarit ry](https://www.otit.fi/), University of Oulu
- [Tampereen TietoTeekkarikilta ry](https://tietoteekkarikilta.fi/), Tampere University
- [Tietokilta ry](https://tietokilta.fi/), Aalto University
